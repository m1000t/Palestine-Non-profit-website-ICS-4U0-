Who are we?

Our donation page is designed to make the process of supporting Palestine easy and convenient. Our goal is to make the process of finding a non-profit website for Palestine easier.

What is the problem?

There has been a continuous conflict between Israel and Palestine for a long time. This has resulted in a situation of extreme hardship for a large number of Palestinians, particularly in Gaza and the West Bank. They are experiencing homelessness, inadequate access to food and water, poor medical treatment, and a lack of safe places to live. The fact that individuals are getting harmed or worse including children is the saddest aspect. This is a serious issue, and you are needed to help uncover answers, assist those in need, and improve the situation for everyone in the area.

What can you do to help?

As a platform dedicated to promoting causes we acknowledge the importance of addressing the current crisis impacting Palestinians. By clicking on the donation tab and choosing any one of the 5 website links to make a donation, you allow us to direct you to groups that actively work to help individuals impacted by the complicated war in Palestine by giving them food, clean water, housing, medical attention, and essential aid. By making a donation, you are directly assisting these vital humanitarian rights and advancing communication of the problems encountered by everybody concerned.



Feel free to contact us about any questions and donation help!
